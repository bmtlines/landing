Current train models

These are four of the most run subway models. The letter lines have wider trains than the number lines.

The R46 has been running since 1975, and is planned to be phased out of service by the end of the decade.

The R62 has been running since 1985, and is mostly used on the local number lines.

The R142 is a more recent version of the R62 as it has been running since 2000 and is mostly used on the express number lines.

The R179 is the most recent subway car as it was introduced into the system in 2017. It is widely used on the letter lines.
